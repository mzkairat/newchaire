Frequently Asked Questions

How are the chairs sourced? ▼
We work directly with corporate liquidation firms that handle office furniture from companies after downsizing. The chairs come from professional office environments and are typically under 1 year old.
What does "like new" mean exactly? ▼
Each chair passes a 25-point inspection. We look for cosmetic issues, test all adjustments, clean the mesh, and lubricate moving parts. "Like new" means you'll receive a fully functional chair with no visible damage.
Why is payment in USDT? ▼
We sell internationally and USDT (USD Tether) is the most stable and widely accessible payment method for cross-border transactions. The TRC20 network means zero transfer fees for you.
How fast will my chair arrive? ▼
Once your USDT payment is confirmed (usually within minutes), we process and ship your order within 24 hours. Delivery takes 1–3 business days across the USA and Canada via express freight.
Do you ship outside the USA and Canada? ▼
We currently ship only to the United States (all 50 states) and Canada. Shipping is 100% free — no hidden fees or duties.
How does the 5-year guarantee work? ▼
If any mechanical component fails within 5 years of purchase, contact us with your order ID and a photo/video of the issue. We will diagnose the problem and ship the necessary replacement parts at no cost.
